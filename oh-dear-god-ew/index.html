OH dear god.. EW!

Published on Jun 19, 2008 Rants! « Prev Next »

I go on a lot of walks here in my neighborhood in order to unwind for the day.  I also like to delude myself and say its exercise but lets be honest, 3 miles every other day aint going to do much.

While out on one of my walks I ran across a 40oz bud light beer can that some asshat had probably tossed out of his car, laying on the side of the road.  I didn’t pay it any mind and kept walking but something about it was out of the ordinary so I actually turned around and walked back to look..  It was a can of “Chelada”.

My immediate and audible response was, “Oh dear god..Ew!”..  Yes.. that’s right boys and girls.. thats bud light and clamato juice, in the same can.  Evidently its been out for about a year now but that is the first time I have seen it and it sounds nasty!  I mean the last damn thing I want in my beer is shellfish..

Seriously who drinks this?  This sounds absolutely vile and makes “New Coke” sound like a good idea.. 

I’ll answer my own question actually.. This can had a pretty healthy dose of spanish on it so it may well be a Mexican thing.. but damn.. 

Suddenly it becomes clear - they didn’t want it in their car because it’s nasty..
